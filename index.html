NIOT: Now Is Our Time

GameBCN Fight Covid-19 Game Jam

Mobile Game for Android (Unity)

Single player mobile videogame. Developed for educational purposes, this game includes an introduction to health and safety measures regarding Covid19, represented in diagrams and fairly visual explanations.
After this informative part, the player will be taken to the main menu. The game consists of some levels with different activities, all regarding Covid's health and care measures: drag and drop masks to the people that spawns on the level, separate them for the minimum safety distance...
Visuals are entirely made up of pixel art.

Game developer / Artist
